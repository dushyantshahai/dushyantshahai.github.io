MorphEd
Transforming Education with 'Assessment as a Service'
What if assessment creation went from 2 hours to 60 seconds — with zero hallucinations?
The Problem
Professors were stuck in a broken loop — manual MCQ creation took hours, direct LLM prompting produced hallucination-prone outputs ungrounded in the actual syllabus, and competitor tools still required multiple review cycles before anything was usable.
The Solution & MVP
A RAG-first architecture: ingest a textbook, map it to a 4-level content hierarchy, generate contextually grounded MCQs on demand with difficulty tuning. The MVP delivers multi-tenant knowledge bases, automated grading, and batch/individual analytics that close the teaching loop end-to-end.

RemindMe
AI Conversational Personal Productivity App
Forms kill the habit of reminders. What if you could just say what you need?
The Problem
Every reminder app starts with a form: pick a date, type a title, set a time. The friction is exactly why people don't set reminders when they actually need to — the moment passes before the setup is complete.
The Solution & MVP
RemindMe replaces the form with a conversation. A custom ConversationManager state machine extracts urgency, theme tags, and time resolution from plain language input — then fires precise push notifications. The MVP delivers Google OAuth, persistent reminders, and real-time push. The vision: a productivity layer that learns your context, not just your calendar.
Stack — Dual-LLM pipeline · Gemini 2.5 Flash · Claude 3 Haiku (fallback) · Firebase push · PostgreSQL · Google OAuth · Vercel
